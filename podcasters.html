Is this actually free? What's the catch?

No catch. Podcast hosts are free forever. Guests pay $249/month to be on the platform and pitch shows. You never pay anything — not now, not when we grow, not ever. We built the business model this way intentionally.

What kind of guests will pitch my show?

Coaches, consultants, agency owners, and thought leaders who have invested in being here and are serious about delivering value. Every guest has a complete media kit and has been filtered by topic relevance to your audience profile.

Do I have to book every guest who pitches me?

Absolutely not. You have full control. Review every pitch on your own schedule and only accept guests who are genuinely the right fit for your audience. Decline, ignore, or accept — it's entirely up to you.

How do I know guests will be prepared?

Every guest on PitchThePod has paid to be here and is actively trying to build their brand through podcast appearances. That investment alone filters out people who aren't serious. Their full talking points and bio are available before you say yes.

What if I don't get pitches that fit my show?

You can update your show profile and audience description at any time to fine-tune the kinds of guests who reach you. Our matching gets smarter the more specific you are about your audience.

How is this different from other podcast guest platforms?

Most platforms charge hosts a fee and show you a database you have to dig through. PitchThePod flips it — guests come to you, pre-vetted, with their pitch already written. Zero work on your end. Zero cost.
